Bread Academy

I learned to bake on my own. And, in fact, it was difficult. I wasted a lot of pain and time. And if it weren't for a man who believed in me and supported me, I would have given up on everything. But then a European woman took me by the hand and carefully led me through the bread lessons, teaching me the art of bread. Now I will do it with you. Because homemade bread is the best therapy and the best way to bring the family together.
